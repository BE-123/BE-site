Arun Gupta

Founder, Beta Endeavors

LinkedIn Email

Technology leader. Ecosystem builder. Strategic advisor.

Arun Gupta is a seasoned technology executive, open source champion, and strategic advisor with over two decades of experience building and scaling developer communities, enterprise platforms, and global technology organizations.

Arun has held leadership roles at some of the world's most influential technology companies — including AWS, Couchbase, and Red Hat — where he drove developer relations, cloud strategy, and product evangelism at a global scale. His work has helped define how modern enterprises adopt open source, cloud-native, and distributed technologies.

Known for his ability to translate deep technical expertise into compelling strategic narratives, Arun has spoken at hundreds of conferences worldwide and built large, engaged developer communities from the ground up. He brings a rare combination of hands-on engineering background and executive-level perspective to every engagement.

Through Beta Endeavors, Arun advises founders, investors, and enterprise teams on technology strategy, go-to-market approaches, developer ecosystems, and organizational design — helping them move faster, think more clearly, and build with greater intention.

He is passionate about open source software, community-led growth, and the transformative power of putting the right tools in the hands of developers.
